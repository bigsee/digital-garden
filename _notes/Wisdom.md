title: Wisdom

> *"By three methods we may learn wisdom: the first, by reflection, which is the noblest; the second, by imitation, which is the easiest; and the third, by experience, which is the bitterest."* - Confucius

As teams, how do we acquire collective wisdom? How do we reflect on our experience? How do we learn from our failures? When (and how) do we choose to imitate?
