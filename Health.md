The *psyche* (mind and soul) and the *soma* (or 'body') are an inseparable unity, despite modern medical attempts to separate them. This is the original and true meaning of the term *psychosomatic*. It has come to be used as a term of derision or dismissal, but it ought to be anything but.

> Social and psychic factors play a role in every disease, but in many conditions, they represent dominant influences. ... Mental factors represent as active a force in the treatment of patients as chemical and physical agents.
> 
	- Dr. Soma Weise, Journal of the American Medical Association (1939)

A related overarching concept is that of *bodymind*, coined by Candace Pert.

> Unless we can measure something, science won't concede it exists, which is why science refuses to deal with such "nonthings" as the emotions, the kind, the soul, or the spirit

	 - Candace Pert, Molecules of Emotions
